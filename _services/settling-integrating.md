        <h1 class="govuk-heading-xl">Settling and Integrating in Minnesota</h1>
        <p class="govuk-body-l">
          This guide is for immigrants. It contains information and advice to help you settle in and successfully integrate.
        </p>
      </div>
    </div>


    <div class="govuk-grid-row">
      <div class="govuk-grid-column-two-thirds">

        <p class="govuk-body"> To facilitate your integration into Minnesota society, it is important to embrace and respect the state's key values. These can be grouped around four main orientations that make Minnesota unique:</p>

        <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
          <li>Minnesota is an English- and Swedish-speaking state.</li>
          <li>Minnesota is a democratic state.</li>
          <li>Men and women are treated equally.</li>
          <li>Minnesota is a secular state.</li>
        </ul>

        <!-- Community Integration -->
        <h2 class="govuk-heading-l govuk-!-margin-top-8">Community Integration</h2>

        <details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
          <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
            <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
              <strong>Understanding Minnesota culture and customs</strong>
            </span>
          </summary>
          <div class="govuk-details__text">
            <p class="govuk-body">
              Minnesota culture is influenced by Swedish and Scandinavian heritage, Midwestern values, and progressive attitudes.
            </p>

            <h3 class="govuk-heading-s">"Minnesota Nice"</h3>
            <p class="govuk-body">
              Minnesotans are known for being polite and reserved. This can sometimes make it challenging to form close friendships quickly.
            </p>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>People are helpful but may not immediately invite you into their social circles</li>
              <li>Minnesotans often avoid direct confrontation</li>
            </ul>

            <h3 class="govuk-heading-s govuk-!-margin-top-4">Social customs:</h3>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Being on time is important and expected</li>
              <li>Maintain comfortable distance in conversations</li>
              <li>Speak softly in public places</li>
              <li>Bragging is generally frowned upon</li>
            </ul>

          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
          <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
            <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
              <strong>Finding community and social connections</strong>
            </span>
          </summary>
          <div class="govuk-details__text">
            <p class="govuk-body">
              Building a social network in a new place takes effort, but Minnesota offers many opportunities to connect.
            </p>

            <h3 class="govuk-heading-s">Ways to meet people:</h3>

            <p class="govuk-body"><strong>Through interests and hobbies:</strong></p>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Join sports leagues (hockey, volleyball, kickball, softball)</li>
              <li>Attend meetup groups (book clubs, hiking groups, language exchanges)</li>
              <li>Take classes (cooking, art, fitness, continuing education)</li>
              <li>Join outdoor clubs (skiing, kayaking, cycling)</li>
            </ul>

            <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-margin-top-4"><strong>Through community organizations:</strong></p>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Religious or spiritual communities</li>
              <li>Volunteer organizations</li>
              <li>Neighborhood associations</li>
              <li>Parent-teacher organizations (PTO/PTA)</li>
              <li>Cultural organizations</li>
            </ul>

            <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-margin-top-4"><strong>For families:</strong></p>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Connect with other parents at schools and daycare</li>
              <li>Join parent groups or mom/dad groups</li>
              <li>Attend library story times and children's programs</li>
              <li>Visit local parks and playgrounds</li>
              <li>Enroll kids in activities (music, sports, scouts)</li>
            </ul>

        <!-- Practical Living -->
        <h2 class="govuk-heading-l govuk-!-margin-top-8">Practical Living in Minnesota</h2>

        <details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
          <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
            <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
              <strong>Navigating winter and seasonal changes</strong>
            </span>
          </summary>
          <div class="govuk-details__text">
            <p class="govuk-body">
              Minnesota winters are long and cold. Preparation and the right attitude will help you not just survive, but enjoy winter.
            </p>

            <div class="govuk-warning-text">
              <span class="govuk-warning-text__icon" aria-hidden="true">!</span>
              <strong class="govuk-warning-text__text">
                <span class="govuk-visually-hidden">Important</span>
                Wind chill can make it feel much colder than the actual temperature. Frostbite can occur in minutes at -20°F wind chill.
              </strong>
            </div>

            <h3 class="govuk-heading-s">Winter essentials:</h3>

            <p class="govuk-body"><strong>Clothing:</strong></p>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Warm winter coat (rated to -20°F or colder)</li>
              <li>Insulated, waterproof boots with good traction</li>
              <li>Warm hat that covers ears</li>
              <li>Insulated gloves or mittens (mittens are warmer)</li>
              <li>Scarf or neck gaiter</li>
              <li>Layers: thermal underwear, fleece, wool</li>
            </ul>

            <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-margin-top-4"><strong>Home:</strong></p>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Snow shovel and ice melt</li>
              <li>Insulated windows or plastic film</li>
              <li>Programmable thermostat</li>
              <li>Humidifier (indoor air gets very dry)</li>
              <li>Emergency supplies (flashlight, batteries, blankets)</li>
            </ul>

            <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-margin-top-4"><strong>Vehicle:</strong></p>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Winter tires or all-season tires in good condition</li>
              <li>Ice scraper and snow brush</li>
              <li>Emergency kit (blanket, flashlight, snacks, water)</li>
              <li>Jumper cables or battery pack</li>
              <li>Kitty litter or sand (for traction if stuck)</li>
              <li>Remote starter (highly recommended!)</li>
            </ul>

            <h3 class="govuk-heading-s govuk-!-margin-top-4">Winter activities to embrace:</h3>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Ice skating on outdoor rinks</li>
              <li>Cross-country skiing or snowshoeing</li>
              <li>Ice fishing</li>
              <li>Downhill skiing and snowboarding</li>
              <li>Winter festivals (Saint Paul Winter Carnival, Ice Castle)</li>
              <li>Hockey (watching or playing)</li>
            </ul>

            <div class="govuk-inset-text">
              <strong>Tip:</strong> The saying goes, "There's no such thing as bad weather, only bad clothing." Dress appropriately and you'll be fine!
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>

            <h3 class="govuk-heading-s govuk-!-margin-top-4">Driving tips:</h3>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Slow down in winter conditions</li>
              <li>Keep extra distance between vehicles</li>
              <li>Accelerate and brake gently on ice</li>
              <li>Use your headlights in snow</li>
              <li>Clear all snow from your car before driving</li>
              <li>If you skid, steer in the direction you want to go</li>
            </ul>

            <h3 class="govuk-heading-s govuk-!-margin-top-4">Alternative transportation:</h3>
            <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
              <li>Biking (extensive trail systems, less practical in winter)</li>
              <li>Ride sharing (Uber, Lyft)</li>
              <li>Car sharing (Zipcar, hourly rentals)</li>
              <li>Walking (skyways connect downtown buildings in winter)</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </details>
