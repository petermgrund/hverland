## The Iron Range

### About the Iron Range
Minnesota's historic mining region in the northeast, including:
- Hibbing
- Virginia
- Eveleth
- Grand Rapids
- Chisholm
- Mountain Iron
- Babbitt
- Ely
- And surrounding communities
