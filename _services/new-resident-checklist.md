        <h2 class="govuk-heading-l">Common questions</h2>

        <details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
          <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
            <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
              Do I need to retake driving tests for a Minnesota license?
            </span>
          </summary>
          <div class="govuk-details__text">
            If you have a valid out-of-state license, you typically only need to pass a vision test and written knowledge test. Road tests are usually not required for license transfers.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
          <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
            <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
              How do I register to vote if I just moved to Minnesota?
            </span>
          </summary>
          <div class="govuk-details__text">
            You can register online, by mail, or on Election Day. You'll need to have lived in Minnesota for at least 20 days before an election to vote in it. You'll need proof of residence (utility bill, lease, etc.).
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
          <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
            <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
              What documents do I need to get a Minnesota driver's license?
            </span>
          </summary>
          <div class="govuk-details__text">
            You'll need your current out-of-state license, proof of identity (passport or birth certificate), proof of Social Security number, and proof of Minnesota residency (utility bill, lease, etc.).
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
          <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
            <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
              How much does vehicle registration cost in Minnesota?
            </span>
          </summary>
          <div class="govuk-details__text">
            Registration fees vary based on the vehicle's age and value. The fee includes registration tax, plate fees, and filing fees. Use the online calculator or contact DVS for an estimate.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="govuk-details" data-module="govuk-details">
          <summary class="govuk-details__summary">
            <span class="govuk-details__summary-text">
              What winter items do I really need as a new Minnesotan?
            </span>
          </summary>
          <div class="govuk-details__text">
            Essential winter items include: a warm winter coat rated for -20Â°F or below, insulated boots with good traction, warm gloves or mittens, a winter hat, ice scraper and snow brush for your car, emergency car kit with blanket, and a good snow shovel for your home.
